The answer is: B

This is a very tricky situation, and it can be hard to resist the desire to help someone who is struggling. This is especially true if you've become close to them in any way.

However, it is important to remember that being a moderator means you represent the freeCodeCamp organisation. As such, great care needs to be taken in situations such as this. Our moderators are not qualified, or expected to be qualified, to handle such situations. It is best to suggest the user seek qualified help, while remaining kind and professional.

Were you to get involved, and the situation took a turn for the worse, not only could you be liable but freeCodeCamp could be found responsible as well. This is why it is important to leave such situations to people who are qualified - if you have resources to help, it is good to share those with the user. But avoid becoming personally involved.
