The answer is: A

In this case, because the profanity was neither excessive nor directed at another user (as an insult, for example), disciplinary action is not necessary. Instead, a simple friendly reminder to follow the rules is sufficient.

While it would also be correct to DM the user (option C), this can feel more heavy handed, especially with newer users. Because of the personal nature of a DM, the user may feel that they are being watched directly. Remember that we are not here to "police" the community, and we want to remain friendly and approachable. This is why it is important to be gentle when possible, but firm when necessary.
