Welcome to the Moderator Training

Being a moderator can be a sizeable undertaking, and there are many nuances for how to best moderate a community. This webpage will walk you through some scenarios to help you understand how to handle different situations. The examples provided are intended to give a general idea for improving your moderation skills, but they are not the holy grail. If you are unsure of how to tackle a specific incident, do not hesitate to reach out to a fellow moderator for guidance or feedback. We are a team, and we are all here to support each other.
